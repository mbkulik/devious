MovieStreamer streams HTML5 compatible video files from your computer to any device with a compatible browser.
Just navigate to the machines address on port 8080 to begin viewing

The following browsers have been known to work:
  • Google Chrome
  • Chromium
  • Opera
  • Firefox
  • Safari
  • iOS browser
  • Android browser
