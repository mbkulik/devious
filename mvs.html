MovieStreamer


This is the first alpha release of MovieStreamer. What is MovieStreamer?
It is a standalone app that streams the HTML5 compatible video files from your
~/Movies directory. Just navigate to http://127.0.0.1:8080 with your perfered browser

The following browsers have been known to work:
  • Chrome/Chromium
  • Firefox
  • Opera
  • Safari
  • Mobile Safari
  • Android
